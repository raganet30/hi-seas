Overview

Hi-Seas Philippines began its operations in June 2019 with market research, followed by the establishment of its first business unit in July 2019 (Non-Trenching). The company was officially registered as HI-SEAS CO. LTD. INC. in September 2019. Barangay Broadband Project

The Barangay Broadband Project is a community-centered initiative aimed at delivering reliable and affordable internet connectivity to underserved rural areas

Utilizing a hybrid GPON and 4G access system, the project ensures rapid deployment through 4G technology during the initial phase, while transitioning to high-speed fiber (GPON) as infrastructure becomes available.
Key features include:
  • Village-wide enterprise-grade Wi-Fi coverage for seamless connectivity.
  • Voucher-based internet access via village kiosks, replacing traditional coin-operated machines.
  • Channel-based distribution model for selling affordable recharge cards.

The project begins with a quick signal survey and community meetings with barangay officials. After the consultation, a detailed site design is created using CAD software, followed by implementation and community-based marketing.
